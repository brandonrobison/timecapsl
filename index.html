the timecapsl story

In late 2015, timecapsl co-founder Brandon Robison learned that his family had paid to have his grandfather professionally interviewed in 1982. The interview had focused on organizing his life experiences into a narrative that could be saved and shared with family members beyond his years. Because his grandfather had passed away when Brandon was only six years old, his curiosity was piqued, and he wanted to learn more about this important man that he had hardly known. However, curiosity quickly turned to disappointment as Brandon's father informed him that the interview had been recorded on cassette tapes and he was no longer sure if a copy still existed.

Brandon would not be deterred. He began contacting family members in his home state of Nevada. After many dead ends, he was extremely excited to learn that one of his aunts possessed a copy of the interview. While he was ecstatic to have discovered a copy of his grandfather's interview, his aunt was unwilling to ship the tape to him out of fear of losing this family treasure in the process. Shortly after, Brandon boarded a plane bound for Las Vegas and then drove several more hours to meet his aunt to pickup the tape.

After Brandon retrieved the recording, he began to listen to it and learned more about his grandfather. Given the age of the tape and the technology used, there were significant issues with the audio. Brandon transferred the interview to his computer and started doing what he could to clean it up and preserve his grandfather's life story to a more permanent format. And that's when he was struck with an idea...

Brandon shared his story and idea with his friend, and fellow co-founder, Elliott Griffin. Elliott had recently lost the only grandfather he had ever known in his life. Soon after that, he and his wife had celebrated the birth of their first child, a little girl named Evelyn. Elliott was saddened by the fact that Evelyn would never meet her great-grandfather, someone who had been such an important person in his own life. He had long regretted not taking advantage of the time they had together to capture his grandfather's amazing life story.

Brandon found closeness to a relative he had hardly known through an amazing set of circumstances, while Elliott recognized that he had missed an opportunity to share one of the most important people in his life with his daughter. In the gap between those two unique experiences, the necessity for a service that provides professional preservation of individual and family histories was revealed. Brandon and Elliott quickly set to work and the result of their collaboration is timecapsl.
